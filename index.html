How it works: Seekers
Answer questions about:
How much you want to get paid
Your skills
Your experience and education
Your personality
The benefits you want
Let our algorithm do the searching for you
Just like on a dating website, we give you a list of your best matches. Browse through their profiles and send connection requests to the jobs you like best. If they like you too, we’ll send them your contact info and let them take it from there.
Let employers come to you
Employers see the same match score you do. So, after creating a profile, you can just sit back and relax. We’ll notify you via email as soon as they reach out to you.
No cover letters ever again
We limit you to three applications per day. This way, when you apply for a job, employers will know that you’re sincerely interested in them.
Only hear from the jobs that value you
Employers are charged a small fee every time they send you a contact request. They're literally paying to talk to you.
The jobs you see will be able to afford you
Enter a minimum salary and we’ll filter out the jobs that offer less. Need dental? You can filter on benefits too.
Job seekers pay nothing
Ever.
How it works: Employers
Answer questions about:
The skills you’re looking for
The experience and education you’re looking for
The cultural and personality attributes you want
The compensation and benefits you offer
Let our algorithm do the searching for you
Just like on a dating website, we give you a list of your best matches. Browse through their profiles and send contact requests to the job seekers you like best. If they like you too, we’ll send you their contact info and let you take it from there.
Let job seekers come to you
Seekers see the same match score you do. We’ll notify you via email once they send you contact requests. You can just sit back and wait.
Only hear from serious candidates
Seekers are limited to three applications per day. If they respond to your post, they’re more interested in you than they are in just about everyone else.
You’ll be able to afford the seekers you see
Enter a maximum salary and we’ll filter out the job seekers that want more.
Filter on skills
Absolutely need someone who knows both inside and enterprise sales? Just make them required skills and you’re good to go.
We’re cheaper than a caramel macchiato
Creating a profile and browsing through your matches is completely free! We’ll also give you two free credits just for entering your credit card info. Thereafter, it’s pay-as-you-go: $5 for every connection request you send or receive. Interested in bulk credits? Email us at contact@amployer.com.
